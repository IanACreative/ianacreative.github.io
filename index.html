Ian Anderson

Cinematographer and gaffer based in San Francisco, California.

Ian works in the camera and lighting departments for commercial, documentary and narrative projects. He recently was gaffer for a Discovery TV miniseries about Alcatraz. He is the cinematographer for "The Hazy Pink Door", which was awarded "Best Film" at the 2024 Butte Film Festival. He is based in the Bay Area but travels nationwide for work, including to LA and NYC.
