About Me

I'm a cinematographer and photographer with a wide scope of interests and experience. I shot "The Hazy Pink Door", which won "Best Film" at the 2024 Butte Film Festival, and my film "LIZ" won "Best Comedy" at the 2022 Grizzly Film Festival. I have worked with companies like Luxury Magazine, Kino Provada, and Free the Youth, and hold a bachelors in Film from UC Santa Cruz.
