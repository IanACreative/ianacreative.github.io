Should Jeremy Go to College?


My Senior Thesis Documentary is currently being sent to fesivals! Catch it here:

"Should Jeremy Go to College?" is a three-month journey filled with interviews, poems, crises, and nostalgia. It concerns anyone who's dealt with the massive confusions that come with considering one's future. Should I follow my heart? Should I study for a career? Should I just sell out? In this project I ask such questions to my professors, peers, and myself.
